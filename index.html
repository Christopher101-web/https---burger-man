Our Latest Specials

The Double Decker Burger

You had better be hungry when you order this burger, you may not eat for a day or 2 afterwards!

This is a serious burger that we invented in order to satisfy a huge hunger! This burger contains double 250 gram beef patties, 2 slices of crispy bacon, a fried egg, 2 slices of cheese, a tamato slice, lettuce, pickles, and our secret sauce, plus a side of chips.

Our Price: R85

The Cheesy Burger

This delicious burger contains a combination of three different cheese's to tease your taste buds. For those who just love cheese, who doesn't?.

For this burger we chose 3 different types of cheese, these being, cheddar, mozzarela, and gouda. You have the option of double or triple patties here, with all the usual trimmings too! We are running a limited time offer on this burger! Served with a side of chips.

Price: Cheesy burger only: R75 | Cheesy burger and Double Decker Burger, only: R150.
